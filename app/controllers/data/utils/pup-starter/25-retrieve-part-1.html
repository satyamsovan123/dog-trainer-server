Retrieve Part 1

Games you can play with Shadow are going to be key parts of building your relationship and having fun together.

You will need:

  • Soft Treats
  • Toys

Steps

  1. While you are interacting with Shadow, roll or gently throw his toy a little bit away from him and see what he does.
  2. Shadow will probably follow it and maybe pick it up.
  3. If he does, encourage him to come back to you with it.
  4. When he does (even if he only comes a little way back) either throw it again (if he drops it) or swap it for a treat (or another toy) and then throw it again.
  5. Repeat this five times.
  6. Introduce this into your play times.

Tips

  • If Shadow doesn’t chase the toy, it's okay, he will get the fun of it soon. Find a more interesting toy and try again later. Squeaky toys or ones that are softer to carry might work better.
  • Make sure you are sounding happy and chirpy when you encourage Shadow to come back!
  • If he doesn’t come back to you, don’t chase him or try to grab the toy - instead move away from him. Shadow is more likely to come to you to show you what he has got!
  • Unless Shadow drops the toy, always swap it for a treat or another similar toy - you don’t want to encourage him to run away from you when he has a ‘prize’ you might not want him to have. Like your shoes!

Retrieve Games

  1. In this lesson, play retrieve games with Shadow.
  2. Shadow should bring a toy back to you when you throw it, most of the time.
  3. You should be able to swap the toy for another toy or a treat.
  4. If Shadow isn't quite there yet, review previous lessons in the library.

You will need:

  • Soft Treats
  • Toys

Steps

  1. Stand in the middle of the room with one toy in your hand and the other behind your back.
  2. Get Shadow interested in the toy you have in your hand and throw it a little way across the room so he chases it.
  3. When he brings it back, run away a few steps so he follows you.
  4. When he gets to you, get him interested in the other toy.
  5. Shadow will drop the first toy, which will be your cue to throw the new one.
  6. When Shadow goes to get it, pick up the first toy.
  7. Repeat as many times as you want. Most German Shepherd Dogs love this game, so don't hesitate to make it extra fun!
  8. This is a game you can play in the garden as well, so Shadow can get used to doing this outdoors.

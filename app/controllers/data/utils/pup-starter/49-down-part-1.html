Down Part 1

You're doing great so far! The ‘Down’ is the next obedience-type exercise you will teach Shadow - another exercise that comes under the heading of ‘good manners.’ This is a great exercise to teach since we're sure there will be many times when you will want Shadow to lie quietly beside you - perfect when taking him round to the pub or out to a café.

You will need

  • Soft Treats

Steps

  1. Start this exercise from a ‘Sit’ position as Shadow is already halfway to lying down.
  2. Hold a treat in your hand by Shadow’s nose and get him interested in it.
  3. Bring your hand slowly down to the ground, slightly between his front paws. Shadow’s nose will follow your hand down.
  4. Hold the treat on the floor while he tries to get at it.
  5. Be patient! It might take some time to get a response as well as a bit of trial and error for where to put the treat.
  6. Eventually, Shadow will lie down to try and get at the treat because it’s just more comfortable for him.
  7. The instant he lies down, say your reward word (“Good”). Great job!
  8. Give Shadow the treat immediately and repeat 5 times.

Tips

  • Try covering the treat slightly with your hand. You might get nibbled and scratched a bit, but it does make it much easier!
  • If Shadow seems to lose focus, that’s totally fine and very normal! Just let him wander off. You can always get him back with the treat and try again later.
  • Remember to be patient! This is a new skill for you both, so it’s no problem at all if it takes a little while to learn.

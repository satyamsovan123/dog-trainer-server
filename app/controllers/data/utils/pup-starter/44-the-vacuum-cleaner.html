Introducing Shadow to the Vacuum Cleaner

Vacuum cleaners aren't very popular among many dogs...some will try to play with it, while others want to chase it, and some are just really scared of it. Our mission is to teach Shadow to ignore them and be happy and comfortable around them, too. To get there, the first step is to have him meet it while it is turned off.

You will need

  • Soft Treats
  • Vacuum cleaner

Steps

  1. Make sure your vacuum cleaner is in the room you are going to work in with Shadow.
  2. Let Shadow come into the room.
  3. As for you, just ignore the vacuum cleaner. If Shadow is curious and feels like sniffing or investigating it, let him.
  4. Once Shadow has finished inspecting it, get his attention and practise some 'Watch Me' exercises to give him something else to think about.
  5. Drop the treat on the floor beside you when Shadow looks at you.
  6. Do this in different parts of the room so he gets used to paying attention to you around the vacuum cleaner.
  7. You can push the vacuum cleaner out of the room after a couple of repetitions to let Shadow see that the vacuum cleaner moves.

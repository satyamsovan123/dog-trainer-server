Recall Part 5

This lesson will teach Shadow to recall from all different parts of the garden so that you can be ready to take it into the "real world". How exciting! If your puppy can’t go outside due to vaccinations just yet, you can always practise in different areas of the house. Is Shadow’s recall a bit hit-and-miss? Pop back to the library a review of previous lessons.

You will need

  • Soft Treats
  • Treat pouch
  • 2m Training Lead
  • Harness

Steps

  1. Put the harness and lead on Shadow and go out into the garden.
  2. Let Shadow move away to the end of the lead and wait until he is not distracted by a good sniff (this can be tricky - remember he is a German Shepherd Dog!).
  3. Say his name and the recall cue word "come”.
  4. When he comes to you, reward him with a treat.
  5. Move backwards as Shadow is coming towards you - this will make it even more fun for him.
  6. Repeat this 5 times in different parts of the garden so it remains in his memory.

Tips

  • Start in a spot where there are no distractions. Then, build up to doing it everywhere in the garden - no matter what is happening!
  • Take this slowly as he will be very easily distracted by interesting smells.
  • Remember the very first exercise you did with Shadow: "Watch Me". If Shadow isn’t looking at you, then you know he isn’t listening.
  • Make sure you have his attention before asking him to come to you to make this as effective as possible.

Play Biting

Puppies use their teeth to play with their littermates, but it's important to teach Shadow that when playing with humans, biting is not really a thing. Today, you will learn how to teach Shadow exactly this - how not to use his teeth when he gets excited playing with you. Painless play times ahead!

You will need

  • Soft Treats
  • Long soft toy

Steps

  1. If Shadow starts to get too excited and bitey, first try to direct him onto a toy to bite instead. Make sure that you are using a toy that Shadow really likes, and that is long enough that your hands aren't too close to those super sharp teeth.

  2. If Shadow is still biting, try wiggling the toy along the floor, alternating between fast and slow movements. This helps make the toy really interesting for Shadow and helps him to stop biting you a bit more easily.

  3. You can also distract him from biting you by giving him something more rewarding to do instead. For example, you might want to practise some 'sits', and giving Shadow a reward each time.

  4. If he is still quite passionate about biting, get up and walk away for a couple of minutes. By doing this, you are teaching Shadow that if he wants fun games and interactions to continue, then using teeth can't come into the picture.

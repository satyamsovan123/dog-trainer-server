Being alone Part 3

Teaching Shadow that he can’t always follow you is an important step towards making them more independent. You’ve already made a great start with this, so now it’s time to take it one stage further.

You will need

  • Stuffed chew toy
  • Baby Gate

Steps

  1. As mentioned before, use a baby gate in the doorway so Shadow can see where you’ve gone.
  2. Give Shadow a treat that will take him a little longer to eat like a toy with peanut butter smeared on it, or hidden into it can be really helpful (A Kong is a great example).
  3. While he is enjoying the toy, leave the room for 1 minute.

Tips

  • Most German Shepherd Dogs are usually very focused on food and playing with toys so Shadow should hardly notice you’ve left, which is exactly what you want.
  • If he looks worried, only go as far as the other side of the baby gate for the first few times.
  • Don’t stay away for any longer than 1 minute in this first week to avoid going too fast.
  • If for any reason you need to leave Shadow for longer, get someone to stay with him and keep him company.

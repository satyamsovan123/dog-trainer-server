Exploring surfaces

Puppies learn how to interact in the world best by exploring and discovering everything around them. For us, this means that by giving them safe and fun things to investigate through walking on, climbing over, smelling, or listening to, we can help their development process, and build their confidence. It's a team job!

Do this little exercise in the room where Shadow feels the most confident and spends most of his time during the day.

Try and gather items and objects that Shadow can find appealing and exciting to discover. Here are a couple of good examples:

  • Paper or plastic bags
  • Cardboard boxes
  • Cake tins
  • Buckets
  • An aerobic step
  • A fluffy blanket
  • A brush

Then, set up an exploration course, with different objects on the floor for Shadow to discover. Let him explore in any way he wants to for however long he feels happy to. German Shepherd Dogs love to explore!

This is the perfect time to get creative. Something as simple as a towel draped over a cardboard box or a fan blowing over some leaves could be new and exciting for Shadow to go under.

You'll also need to be patient for this one. Some pups are really confident from the start and can't wait to explore, while others take more time. But there's no need to worry if Shadow seems a little wary; puppies who take their time, are often the ones who learn the most!

Tips

  • Add items of different heights for Shadow to stand on like a low box or an aerobic step. This will help him to experience life from another perspective. How poetic.
  • Hide treats and toys inside, underneath, or on top of some of the objects for Shadow to find and be surprised by.
  • Stay close to Shadow. This way, he gets confidence from you being there.
  • Encourage him by using your voice - be sure to keep it joyful and happy!

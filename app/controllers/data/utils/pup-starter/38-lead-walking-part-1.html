Lead Walking - Part 1

If Shadow is already comfortable wearing his harness, it's time to start teaching him how to walk on a loose lead. Keep in mind that this skill takes time for puppies to learn, so don't worry if it's not perfect from the start. You'll have plenty of time to practice this over the next few months, making your walks together much more enjoyable.

You will need:

  • Soft Treats
  • Treat pouch
  • Harness

Steps

  1. Put the harness on Shadow, as you have been practicing.
  2. Hold a treat in your left hand and position Shadow on your left-hand side.
  3. Show Shadow the treat at his nose-height.
  4. Walk forward a few steps, encouraging Shadow to walk beside you.
  5. After a few steps, use your reward word and give Shadow the treat.
  6. Repeat this exercise around 5 times.
  7. If Shadow performs well, try the exercise again by holding the treat in your hand near the top of your left thigh. Then walk forward normally.
  8. Limit the distance to around 6 steps for now, celebrating Shadow's progress!
  9. Repeat the exercise another 5 times and give yourselves a round of applause for the effort.

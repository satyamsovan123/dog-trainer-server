Retrieve Part 2

Fun times ahead, let's teach Shadow to retrieve. In this task, we will work on encouraging Shadow to bring and give the toy back to you. German Shepherd Dogs are quite fond of these kinds of games, so they usually pick them up quite quickly, and it gives you a fun way to play together in return.

You will need

  • Soft Treats
  • Toys

Steps

  1. Roll or gently throw Shadow’s toy a little bit away from him.
  2. This time, when he picks it up, move a few steps backwards to encourage him to follow you with the toy.
  3. Stop after a few steps and when he gets to you, take hold of the toy but don’t tug or pull.
  4. Instead, show him the other toy and wriggle it around to get him more interested in that one.
  5. When Shadow lets go of the first toy, throw the second one. It's all about swapping!
  6. Repeat this 5 times and make sure to incorporate this into your playtimes from now on to help with practice.

Tips

  • Don’t worry if Shadow doesn’t bring the toy all the way back. It's better if you don’t go forward towards him or try to grab it! Just show him the second toy and keep rewarding every attempt.
  • Some puppies take longer to learn that giving you something back means the game continues!
  • If Shadow still won’t let you take hold of the toy, make the second toy much more interesting by making it come alive with funny voices or dragging it on the floor. When he then lets go of the first toy, throw the second one.
  • Be upbeat and enthusiastic when you are playing with Shadow! It will really help to keep him engaged and interested.

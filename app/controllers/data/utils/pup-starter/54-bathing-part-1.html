Bathing Part 1

Puppies are cute, but messy! For times when that infatuating fresh-puppy smell gets clouded and overshadowed with the smell of mud and hints of poo, it’ll be helpful to know how to bathe your puppy. The first stage of how to give puppies a bath is getting them used to the bath area. That’s what we’re focusing on for this lesson.

You will need

  • Stuffed Kong toy
  • Non-slip mat
  • Old towel
  • Licky Mat

Steps

  1. Have your food toy ready, like a likkimat with some yummy paste on or a Kong stuffed with something delicious. This will help Shadow associate the bath or shower (or sink!) with something nice and tasty.
  2. Make sure you have a non-slip bath mat or an old towel at the bottom of the bath or shower so that Shadow won’t slip.
  3. Pop Shadow in the tub or shower while you sit nearby and give them the stuffed food toy or scatter some treats for them to eat.
  4. Let Shadow enjoy the toy and treats, and when they’re finished, get him out. No need to get any water out just yet.

Tips

  • It might not feel like you did much, but creating positive associations before the water goes on is important, and it’s so easy to do!
  • Practise this several times before you go on to the next step.

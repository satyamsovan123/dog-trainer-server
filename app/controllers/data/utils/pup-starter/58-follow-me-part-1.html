Follow me - Part 1

This task will help keep Shadow focussed on you. It does wonders for improving your recall, bonding, and building his confidence as he gets used to having distance between you.

You will need

  • Soft Treats
  • Treat pouch

Steps

  1. First, practise a couple of "sits" to get Shadow’s focus, rewarding each one with a treat.
  2. After you have done a couple, throw the treat a little farther away so that Shadow will have to go and get it.
  3. As Shadow is eating the treat, move a few steps away.
  4. Now, he must come further to get back to you.
  5. Keep repeating this cycle - ask for the sit, throw the reward, and move a few steps away. Increase the distance you move away a little more each time to keep it interesting and sharpen his following skills!

Tips

  • Be enthusiastic! It will help a great deal.
  • Don’t go too far from Shadow to start with...don't want to make it too challenging for him.
  • Don’t move until Shadow is eating the treat. You don’t want him to have to choose between going to get the treat and following you...the ultimate confusion.
  • When you know that Shadow is enjoying this new game, start to run away a little bit. It will make it even more exciting and make you the best companion!

Sit Part 4 - Outdoors

Let's be honest - dogs don't generalize very well. If you always teach Shadow to sit in the kitchen, you may well find he wonâ€™t do it anywhere else. For this task, you'll be teaching Shadow that when you say "sit", it also means "do it everywhere".

You will need

  • Soft Treats
  • A little patience!

Steps

  1. In each of the locations, practice all the behaviors you have taught Shadow already ("sit", "down", and "recall") about three times each to make sure it has stuck.
  2. Each time you go to a new location, start the exercise right back at the very beginning for the first few times. Luring the first one in each new place will really help.

Tips

We don't want to burst your bubble, but you can't expect Shadow to do familiar exercises everywhere correctly at first, even if he can do it easily where you usually train. By teaching him to generalize on all the lessons you do, everything will fall into place nicely.

You will need:

  • Tasty high-value treats
  • Treat pouch
  • 2m Training Lead
  • Harness

Steps

  1. Put the harness and lead on Shadow and go into the garden.
  2. Hold a treat in your left hand at the top of your left thigh.
  3. Walk forward normally, with Shadow walking beside you.
  4. If Shadow gets ahead of you or pulls the lead tight, simply stop! No need to pull on the lead. Instead, use your enthusiastic voice and yummy treats to let Shadow come back to you.
  5. Carry on walking after he comes back, and reward him every 6 steps.
  6. Slowly build up to walking 12 steps.
  7. Vary your speed and direction. You can make this a fun game now that you are outside and have more space.

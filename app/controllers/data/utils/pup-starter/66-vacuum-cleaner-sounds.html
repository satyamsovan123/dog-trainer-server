Vaccum Cleaner Sounds

  1. In this lesson, we’ll be teaching Shadow that the vacuum cleaner makes a noise when it moves.
  2. If Shadow isn’t comfortable with the vacuum cleaner moving around, pop back to the library and review the previous lessons.
  3. Vacuum cleaners can be strange and scary for puppies, so it’s important we teach him they’re nothing to worry about.

You will need

  • Soft Treats
  • Vacuum cleaner

Steps

  1. First, start by letting Shadow hear the noise of the vacuum cleaner from another room for a couple of minutes.
  2. If possible, ask a friend to turn on the vacuum cleaner while you are playing or sitting with Shadow in another room.
  3. If you're alone, leave Shadow with some treats, switch on the vacuum cleaner, and return.
  4. Ignore the vacuum noise and continue what you were doing before it started. This helps Shadow learn that the noise is nothing to worry about.
  5. Wait a few minutes before giving Shadow another treat, then switch off the vacuum cleaner.
  6. If you have a friend, have them use the vacuum cleaner in a nearby room so Shadow can see its movement and hear the noise simultaneously.
  7. If alone, scatter treats again but avoid using the vacuum cleaner close to Shadow just yet. Progress gradually.

Tips

  • Vacuum with Shadow on the other side of a baby gate to avoid frightening him.
  • Avoid moving the vacuum cleaner too quickly or directly towards Shadow initially.

Muffin Tin Puzzle for Dogs

This Muffin Tin Puzzle is easy to set up, and we can pretty much guarantee that Shadow (and you) will have a great time playing with it.

When Shadow uses his nose to find treats, it's excellent mental stimulation and a fantastic opportunity for Shadow to have loads of new experiences like moving things around all kinds of different materials and hearing new, funny noises.

Here's what you'll need:

  • A metal muffin tin.
  • Six tennis balls.
  • Small tasty treats that you know Shadow loves.

There are two ways to play this game. Double the fun!

  1. The first is to place the tin upside down on a non-slip floor or rug. Then, sprinkle the treats between the raised muffin moulds so that Shadow has to sniff, nose, or paw them out.
  2. The second way to play is to set the tin the right way up, and place treats or part of Shadow's meal into the pans, with a tennis ball topping each one off. This time, Shadow will have to nose, nudge, or paw the balls from the tray to reveal the treats.

Either way, Shadow will be filled with excitement, and you with joy as you see him digging in so happily.

Tips

  • Start out quite easy and point out where the treats are to give Shadow some help. He will get the hang of it soon enough.
  • As the game gets harder, use higher-value treats so that Shadow can smell them easily and make the game an even yummier experience.
